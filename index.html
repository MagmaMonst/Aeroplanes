Introduction

Aeroplanes are one of the greatest inventions of modern times, enabling us to travel quickly across the globe. They are fascinating examples of engineering and aerodynamics.

History of Aeroplanes

The first powered flight was achieved by the Wright brothers in 1903. Since then, aeroplanes have evolved from simple wooden structures to sophisticated flying machines.

Types of Aeroplanes

  • Commercial Airliners
  • Private Jets
  • Military Aircraft
  • Cargo Planes

Fun Facts

  • The fastest aeroplane, the SR-71 Blackbird, can travel at over 3,500 km/h.
  • The Airbus A380 is the largest passenger plane in the world.
  • Modern planes can fly for over 15 hours nonstop!
